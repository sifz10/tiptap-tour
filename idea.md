## Ideas
1. Tour Tips and check list
2. Tour guide
3. Dead Reckonining - Last known location : If user lost connection from the group group chat should see the last location of the user.
4. Add a feature for clound sync, Required login. If user want to sync progress into online they can.
5. Photo download feature in Bulk and single and by selecting.
6. Pre-Trip "Gatekeeper" Download: This is crucial. Before the user loses signal, the app must aggressively prompt them to download offline topographical maps, trail data, and the tour group's profile keys. The app should literally block them from entering "Tour Mode" until it verifies these assets are saved locally.

7. Battery-Aware Mesh Routing: Wi-Fi Direct and Bluetooth mesh networks absolutely devour battery life. Your app needs a "Low Power Mesh" feature. If a user's battery dips below 20%, the app should stop acting as a relay node for other people's messages and only transmit the user's own essential location pings.

8. AR (Augmented Reality) Group Finder: Since you have the GPS coordinates of the other tour members via the mesh, use the phone's local gyroscope and camera. A user can hold up their phone, and an AR arrow overlay on the screen points directly toward the group leader or the base camp, showing the distance in meters.

9. Local Emergency Medical Vault: Allow users to input their blood type, allergies, and emergency contacts into a local encrypted vault. If they trigger an SOS via the mesh network, this vital info is bundled into the distress packet and sent to the tour leader's phone.

10. Sunrise/Sunset & "Return to Base" Calculator: The app can calculate local sunset times completely offline based on the phone's GPS coordinates and the calendar date. It can feature a dynamic alarm that warns the group: "Based on your distance from basecamp, you must turn around in 15 minutes to make it back before dark."
